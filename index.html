About QVL

Who We Are

Queensland Valuers Limited (QVL) is a Kenyan-based Property Valuation, Property management, Agency and Consultancy firm established in year 2022. We provide professional, timely and reliable real estate solutions to all our clients.

Our licensed team combines technical expertise with in-depth knowledge of the Kenyan real estate market to deliver services that meet regulatory standards and support informed financial decisions.

At QVL, we are committed to professionalism, integrity, and excellence in every assignment we undertake.

What Sets Us Apart

- Licensed under Kenya’s Valuers and Estate Agents Acts
- Proud members of ISK, VRB, and EARB
- Serving all of Kenya with a regional focus
- Adhering to RICS and IVSC global standards
- Client-first approach with tailored, experience-driven solutions

Our Vision

We aim to be East Africa’s leading partner for professional real estate services.

Our Motto: “Your Concern is Our Priority.”
